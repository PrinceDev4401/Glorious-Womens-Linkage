DEPARTMENT HEAD

Ms. Edem Gloria Akorli

GLOWHAGTOR Head

GLOWHAGTOR

Miss Edem Gloria Akorli is a public servant working as an administrator with one of the nation’s public universities. She is an entrepreneur who owns a business consulting firm and is passionate about girl child education and the livelihood empowerment of women.

The major goal of the organization is to inspire leadership in young girls and raise pioneers. That can be difficult to achieve without education, hence the need for the Glowhagtor wing. We work to drive the importance of education in rural communities within the country that are lacking it. We are excited to achieve consistent growth and success along with the organization.

This department’s activities are geared towards educating girls and empowering them to pursue their academics and become whatever they desire to be. Our activities also include educating the general public on issues affecting the girl child and women.

Our yearly activities include organizing a talk show on education and social issues relating to girls in a selected town or village at the beginning of the year, during which opportunities are given to the girls who fall into these four categories for support:

  • Girls who have dropped out of school or cannot attend due to lack of finances.
  • Girls who are in school but need extra financial support.
  • Girls who have financial support but need mentors to guide them academically.
  • Girls who need help to learn a trade.

GLOWHAGTOR works with them throughout the year by providing what they need based on their category. We also organize forums to educate parents and the public on giving equal opportunities to girls as boys. In addition, we host quarterly educational shows in selected schools or communities to shed light on issues affecting girls and women in all spheres of life.
