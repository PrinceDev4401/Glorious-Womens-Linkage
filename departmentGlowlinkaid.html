DEPARTMENT HEAD

Madam Antoinette Hiagbe

Experienced Teacher & Program Director

GLOWLINK AID (GlowAid)

Madam Antoinette Hiagbe has been working with the Ghana Education Service as a teacher for almost two decades. She believes in making a lasting impact on all children who undergo her tutelage — a passion she brings to her work in leading outreaches and giving exercises within the nation, building both the mind and the soul. She is also a proud board member of this organization.

This department seeks to be a source of help to the needy and less privileged in our society, as stated in the core values of the ministry. As Christians, it is our duty to love all equally, respect the self-worth of every human being, and to help those in need just as Jesus did while on earth.

Over the past few years, GlowAid has organized donation exercises to head porters (kayayei), orphanages, and prisons in Ghana. By doing this, we hope to share the love of God with mankind and cause them to draw closer to Christ as they come to know Him personally.
