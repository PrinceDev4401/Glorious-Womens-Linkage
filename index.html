ABOUT THE CEO AND FOUNDER OF GLOWLINK

Mrs. Rosemond Köhler

Glorious Women’s Linkage (GLOWLINK) is a word-based women’s network established to raise model Christian women leaders serving as role models and mentors to women worldwide. We do this by organizing mentorship programs and conferences annually to empower the youth, particularly young women, single mothers, the needy, and less privileged in societies both locally and nationwide. We are almost in every region in Ghana.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

About Us

Glorious Women's Linkage (GLOWLINK) is a word-based women's network established to raise model Christian women leaders serving as role models and mentors to women worldwide.

Mission

Empowering and raising the next generation of virtuous women with purpose and character, through Nurture, Mentorship, and Principles of the Word of God.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the world's largest Christian women empowerment organization endowed to equip other women with leadership and entrepreneurial skills; commissioned to cater for the needs of the less privileged in society and ordained to empower young people around the world to fulfill their purpose in life.

LEADERSHIP

GLOWLEAD

Glowlead is aimed at nurturing and developing the leadership skills in women worldwide. In Africa and in some cultures, women face high barriers that limit them in attaining the highest levels of power in leadership, education, and business.
