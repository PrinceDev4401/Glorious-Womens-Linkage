About Glorious Women’s Linkage (GLOWLINK)

Glorious Women’s Linkage (GLOWLINK) is a faith-based, women-focused non-governmental organization dedicated to the empowerment, mentorship, and holistic development of girls and women across Ghana, Africa, and beyond.

Founded with a strong passion for transformation, GLOWLINK exists to raise confident, responsible, and purpose-driven women who positively impact their families, communities, and nations. Since its establishment, the organization has directly impacted over 5,000 girls and women through mentorship, education, leadership training, advocacy, and humanitarian service.

What We Do

GLOWLINK addresses critical social and developmental challenges affecting women and vulnerable groups, including:

  • Girl-child education and advocacy against early marriage
  • Mentorship and leadership development
  • Menstrual health and hygiene education
  • Youth empowerment and entrepreneurship training
  • Health awareness, sanitation, and medical outreach
  • Support for widows, single mothers, orphans, prisoners, and the elderly
  • Evangelism and spiritual growth

Our Approach

Through school outreaches, conferences, summits, vocational training programmes, donations, medical screenings, and faith-based activities, GLOWLINK promotes dignity, equality, confidence, and opportunity for women and girls.

The organization operates through specialized departments including GLOWHAGTOR, GLOWAID, and GLOWLEAD, each focused on mentorship, humanitarian aid, leadership development, advocacy, and spiritual empowerment.

Our Impact

GLOWLINK has successfully implemented programmes across multiple regions in Ghana and other African countries, collaborating with institutions such as the Ghana Education Service (GES), National Commission for Civic Education (NCCE), health professionals, community leaders, and faith-based organizations.

Despite challenges such as the COVID-19 pandemic, GLOWLINK adapted by hosting virtual empowerment conferences with global speakers, extending its reach beyond physical borders.

Our Global Vision

Today, GLOWLINK operates in 16 African countries and continues to expand through the recruitment of country directors across the continent. The organization is committed to building a global network of empowered women who will lead with integrity, resilience, compassion, and faith.

GLOWLINK believes that when women are empowered, families are strengthened, communities are transformed, and nations are built.
